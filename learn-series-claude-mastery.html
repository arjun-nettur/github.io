Claude Mastery

A complete guide to understanding and working with Claude — from the basics of how it thinks to advanced prompting, agentic workflows, and responsible use.

AI 30 Chapters Beginner → Advanced 5 of 30 Available

Claude Mastery is your comprehensive guide to working effectively with Claude AI. Whether you're just starting out or looking to master advanced techniques, this series will teach you how to get exceptional results through clear communication, strategic prompting, and thoughtful iteration.

This is a learning-in-public series. As I discover what works (and what doesn't) in real-world use, I'm documenting everything here. The first 5 chapters cover the foundational techniques that transform how you work with Claude. More advanced chapters are coming as the series grows to 30 total chapters.

Learn how to communicate clearly with Claude to get exactly what you need — from emails to complex analysis.

🎯

Systematic Thinking
